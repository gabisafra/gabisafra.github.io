Who We Are...
The initiative of this research project is to develop comprehensive innovation methodologies comprised of new innovation techniques that are both powerful and practical, and to then extend the scope of these insights to new frontiers. We intend to do this by conducting in depth research and interviews with Nobel Laureates, particularly those from UC Berkeley, Stanford University and UCSF, as seek to understand the creative thought processes of Nobel Prize winners and the common principles that lead to innovation.
Our Partners...
Our project is partnered and supported by host of institutions on the edge of innovation and scientific research. We are partnered with the Autodesk Innovation Genome , a research project and innovation methodology being developed at Autodesk's headquarters in San Francisco. We also working in accordance with a research team at Oxford University and in conjunction with the Nobel Foundation in Stockholm, Sweden.
